meet hexIO

 A creative educational toy built by Rice University students to teach children about boolean programming logic.



our vision

As STEM students, we have a passion for sharing our knowledge and inspiring others to pursue their STEM interests. We want HexIO to bridge the gap in pre-college education, and provide kids with a chance to learn more about the world around them.
