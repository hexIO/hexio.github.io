meet hexIO

 A modular toy built by Rice University students to teach children about boolean programming logic.

the product

We've created modular building blocks that contain Boolean logic gates, the pieces in computers that process signals of 0's and 1's. When kids play with our blocks using our logic puzzle curriculum, they learn how a computer actually processes code.



our vision

As current college students studying in STEM fields, we have a passion for sharing our knowledge and inspiring others to pursue STEM interests. We want HexIO to help bridge a current gap in pre-college education, and provide kids a chance to learn more about the world around them.
